INAUGURATION OF ALI HAJVERY FREE DIALYSIS CENTER, GOVT. MIAN MUNSHI HOSPITAL, LAHORE

The goal fixed by Dr Zaheer-ul-Hassan Mir at the time opening of first unit of Ali Hajvery Free Drug Bank in Mayo Hospital, Lahore on March 05,2012 to help the needy patients to provide medicines free of cost and healthcare facilities, the other mile stone is crossed by opening of Ali Hajvery Haemodialysis Centre at Govt. Mian Munshi Hospital, Lahore. To obey the divine order of Almighty ALLAH and his Prophet MUHAMMAD (SAW) to serve the people and comfort to ailing humanity, the third Unit of Ali Hajvery Free Drug Bank is inaugurated by Honourable Khawaja Ahmed Hassan, Special Adviser to Punjab Chief Minister and Mr Bilal Yasin Punjab Food Minister on May 06,2017 with Dr Zahid Pervez D.G. Health (R), Dr Muhammad Ameer M.S. Services Hospital , Lahore, Dr Fakhar-Uz- Zaman M.S. Govt, Mian Munshi Hospital, Lahore, Haji Tariq Patron-in Chief AHFDB and other distinguished guests.
